History of ICCT Colleges

ICCT Colleges was established in 1992 as the Institute of Creative Computer Technology. Founded by Dr. William S. Co, the institution started with a vision of providing quality and affordable education in computer technology and related fields. Through the years, ICCT expanded its academic programs and campuses across Rizal Province, becoming one of the leading educational institutions in the region. Today, ICCT Colleges continues to produce globally competitive graduates equipped with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to meet the demands of the modern world.

Vision

To become the leading premier provider of higher education in Asia.

Mission

To prepare students for the manifold demands of technological efficiency needed in the fields of Information & Communication Technology, the Health Sciences and the various disciplines of educational pursuits through research, advance studies, and international linkages; and to temper this training with the inculcation of genuine love for work and the virtues of a value-based individual.

Founder

Dr. William S. Co

Dr. William S. Co founded the Institute of Creative Computer Technology in 1992. His vision was to provide accessible, affordable, and quality education to students while preparing them for the ever-evolving demands of technology, industry, and society.
