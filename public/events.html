SPORTFEST 2026

The opening ceremony of Sportfest 2026 brought together students, faculty, and staff in a vibrant celebration of school spirit. The parade of teams, lighting of the torch, and presentation of competing colors set the tone for friendly competition and unity across all departments.

Field and court events showcased the athletic talent of ICCT Antipolo students. From basketball and volleyball to track and field races, every participant demonstrated determination, teamwork, and sportsmanship, cheering each other on through every match.

The closing day highlighted the values learned beyond the games: camaraderie, discipline, and respect. Awards were given to outstanding teams and athletes, marking the end of a successful Sportfest 2026 that strengthened bonds and school pride.
